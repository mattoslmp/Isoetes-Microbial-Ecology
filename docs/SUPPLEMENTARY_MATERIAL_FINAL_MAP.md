# Final supplementary material map

The supplementary material was reorganized to avoid repeated labels for different datasets.

## Final table labels

- **Table S1** – sequencing quality summary.
- **Table S2** – differentially abundant MetaCyc pathways across treatments; this workbook is the source used to generate Figure 6.
- **Table S3** – taxonomic differential abundance summary based on NOISeq results.
- **Table S4** – taxonomic composition / abundance summary at phylum, family and genus levels.
- **Table S5** – additional complete MetaCyc enriched-pathway workbook retained for completeness.

## Supplementary figures

- **Figure S1** – taxonomic differential abundance barplots.
- **Figure S2** – PICRUSt2 pathway count distributions and TMM-normalized distributions.
- **Figure S3** – complete all-pathway MetaCyc heatmap. This preserves the original full heatmap, while the manuscript Figure 6 shows a cleaner selected subset of representative pathways.

## Notes

Exact duplicate supplementary files were removed from the final package to avoid repeated nomenclature. When a workbook contained the same data as another file, only one non-duplicated supplementary label was retained.
