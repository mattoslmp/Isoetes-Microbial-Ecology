# Revised Figure 6 caption

**Figure 6. Selected MetaCyc pathways enriched across treatment contrasts based on PICRUSt2-predicted functional profiles.** The heatmap summarizes representative pathways selected from Table S4 and grouped by functional category, including carbon/C1 and energy metabolism, aromatic compound degradation, cofactor and vitamin biosynthesis, terpenoid/isoprenoid metabolism, nitrogen/sulfur metabolism, and stress/iron/cell-envelope processes. Cell values represent fold-change values from the corresponding contrast; blank cells indicate that the pathway was not listed as enriched for that comparison. Starred pathways indicate highlighted processes with strong or biologically informative enrichment, including methanogenesis from H2 and CO2, mevalonate and geranylgeranyl diphosphate biosynthesis, aromatic compound degradation, NAD salvage, sulfolactate degradation, and enterobactin biosynthesis.
